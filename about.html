About Me

I am a sophomore at Carnegie Mellon University studying Information Systems and Human-Computer Interaction. I am interested in UX design, product design, and product management.

Being a highly observant person, I always unconsciously care about and think about the detailed design of things in my everyday life, from a small road sign on the street to the interaction of a website. I found my career interest in UX design and product design in my freshman year after trying to design and build a website on my own. I was intrigued by how people benefit from a user-friendly interaction design, and I feel passionate about designing products and interactions that can make people's life more convenient.
